Huskey

Huskey, known for their wolf-like looks and trademark blue eyes.

  • Nick Name: wolfie
  • Average Weight: 45 lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Hobby: Digging

A medium size working dog breed that originated in north-eastern Siberia, Russia. The breed belongs to the Spitz genetic family. It is recognizable by its thickly furred double coat, erect triangular ears, and distinctive markings.
